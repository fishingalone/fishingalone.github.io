Interpolation Spaces

Basics

Definitions

Let's say there are two normed spaces \( X_0\) and \( X_1\). We call \( X_0\) and \( X_1\) a compatible pair \( X=(X_0,X_1)\) if they are subspaces of some larger vector space. The intersection of the two spaces is a normed space defined with \[ \| u\|_{X_0\cap X_1} := \left( \| u\|_{X_0}^2 + \| u\|_{X_1}^2 \right)^{1/2}. \] The sum of the two spaces is a normed space with \[ \| u\|_{X_0+X_1} := \inf_{\substack{u_0\in X_0\\ u_1\in X_1\\ \text{s.t.} u=u_0+u_1}} \left{ \| u_0\|_{X_0}^2 + \| u_1\|_{X_1}^2 \right}^{1/2}. \]
